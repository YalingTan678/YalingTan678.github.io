## Study Overview

This meta-analysis synthesizes existing research on technology-based educational interventions and their impact on academic achievement for students from low-income families.

## Research Questions

- What is the overall effect of technology-based interventions on academic achievement for low-income students?
- Which types of technology interventions are most effective?
- What moderating factors influence the effectiveness of technology for this population?
