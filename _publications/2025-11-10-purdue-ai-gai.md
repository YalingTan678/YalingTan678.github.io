## Presentation Summary

This mixed-methods study investigates the effects of extramural generative AI (GAI)-mediated Informal Digital Learning of English (IDLE) on the pragmatic and communicative competence of Chinese undergraduates.

## Research Design

- Mixed-methods approach combining quantitative measures of pragmatic competence with qualitative analysis of learner experiences
- Focused on how students engage with GAI tools outside formal classroom settings
- Examined both linguistic outcomes and learner perceptions of AI-mediated practice

## Key Contributions

- Bridged the gap between IDLE research and generative AI applications
- Provided evidence on the effectiveness of GAI for developing pragmatic awareness
- Offered design implications for AI-mediated informal learning environments
