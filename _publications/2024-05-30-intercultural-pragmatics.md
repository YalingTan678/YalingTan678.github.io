## Conference Participation

Attended and participated in the 10th International Conference on Intercultural Pragmatics and Communication, held May 30 – June 1, 2024 in Pisa, Italy. This conference brought together researchers working at the intersection of pragmatics, intercultural communication, and language use in diverse contexts.
