## Study Overview

This paper investigates how higher education instructors navigate the evolving landscape of student writing in the age of generative AI. As AI writing tools become ubiquitous, fundamental questions about authorship, agency, and authenticity are being renegotiated in academic settings.

## Key Themes

- How instructors define and assess authentic student writing when AI tools are available
- The shifting boundaries of authorship in AI-assisted academic work
- Strategies instructors employ to preserve student agency in writing
- Implications for academic integrity policies and pedagogical practice
