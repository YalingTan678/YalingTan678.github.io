## Overview

This study proposes a practice-oriented framework for using Doubao, a generative AI tool, as a scaffold in senior high school English as a Foreign Language (EFL) writing instruction. The framework addresses the challenges of integrating AI into classroom writing practice while ensuring that students develop authentic writing skills.

## Key Contributions

- Developed a structured framework for integrating GenAI scaffolding in EFL writing classrooms
- Demonstrated measurable improvements in student writing performance
- Provided practical guidelines for teachers implementing AI-assisted writing instruction at the secondary level
